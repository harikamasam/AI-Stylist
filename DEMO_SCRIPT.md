# AI Stylist Demo Script

## 0-5 sec: Hero Intro

Open with the landing hero at `https://aistylist.in`.

Narration:
“AI Stylist is a fashion intelligence platform that helps users see their style before they buy.”

Show:
- Premium hero
- Product mock preview
- `Start Styling` CTA

## 5-15 sec: Select Category + Paste Product URL

Click `Start Styling`.

Show:
- Select a category such as `Shirts` or `Shoes`
- Choose a fashion style such as `Minimal` or `Streetwear`
- Paste a product URL into Product Lens

Narration:
“The user starts by selecting a category and pasting a fashion product link.”

## 15-25 sec: AI Mirror + Tracking

Show the AI Mirror.

Narration:
“MediaPipe powers the AI mirror, with tracking modes that adapt to the selected category: torso, face, wrist, or foot tracking.”

Show:
- AI Mirror
- Tracking HUD
- Guided stepper progress

## 25-35 sec: Recommendations + Analytics

Open `Recommendations`, then `Analytics`.

Narration:
“The recommendation engine returns category-aware products, while the analytics layer scores fit, confidence, trend relevance, and outfit match.”

Show:
- Product cards with images, price, brand, match score
- AI Analytics
- Outfit Intelligence

## 35-45 sec: Before/After Comparison

Open Analytics and scroll to the comparison card.

Narration:
“The before/after comparison creates a polished try-on moment for visual storytelling.”

Show:
- BEFORE label
- AI TRY-ON label
- Draggable comparison slider

## 45-55 sec: Aura Stylist Assistant

Open `Assistant`.

Narration:
“Aura Stylist provides rule-based styling guidance for colors, occasions, accessories, and outfit choices.”

Show:
- Assistant welcome message
- Ask a styling question
- Quick prompt response

## 55-60 sec: Closing Screen

Return to the hero or workspace overview.

Closing title:
“Built with React • FastAPI • MediaPipe • Firebase”

Narration:
“AI Stylist combines full-stack engineering, computer vision, recommendation logic, and polished product design into one portfolio-ready fashion SaaS platform.”
